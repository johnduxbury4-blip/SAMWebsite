About

A career built on
strategic rigour

John Duxbury MRICS is a Chartered Surveyor with over 30 years of experience as an investment and asset management specialist, working across both investor and occupier perspectives in UK commercial real estate.

Most recently serving as Director and Head of UK Portfolio Asset Management at M&G Real Estate — one of Europe's largest real estate investment managers — John led the UK asset management team through significant market cycles, consistently delivering investment performance for clients and shareholders across all major commercial sectors.

John brings board-level experience as a Non-Executive Director across a broad range of commercial and living real estate funds, and as Company Director on investor Joint Venture Boards and Asset SPVs. He has served as Chair of Asset Management Committees and as a member of Investment Committees, and is an active contributor to the British Property Federation's Asset Management Committee.

Having completed a distinguished 27-year career at M&G Real Estate, John is now available for NED appointments, trustee board roles, and real estate advisory mandates across asset management, investment, property management, and development.

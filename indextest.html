Specialist Capability

Complex Situations & Dispute Resolution

Some of the most value-destructive moments in a real estate portfolio are not market-driven — they are operational. Technical defects, contractor failures, warranty claims, JV partner disputes, occupier insolvencies and contractual disagreements can consume disproportionate client time over months or years.

This is where Stockbridge Asset Management offers something distinctive — senior commercial leadership that balances legal, financial and reputational considerations, knowing when to push back hard, when to negotiate, and when a pragmatic settlement serves the client's interests better than prolonged conflict.

Working closely with the client to establish a clear brief and objectives, John as a senior client representative can take ownership of the situation, define the strategy and lead and direct the client's external professional and legal advisors alongside internal subject experts.

Dispute resolution strategy across high-value technical defect and warranty claim situations at industrial assets — coordinating multi-party negotiations to reach financial settlements through mediation.

Directed resolution of a major contractor performance dispute on a large mixed-use commercial development — resolved without litigation.

Managed negotiations across more than 20 occupier administration, CVA and restructuring situations — working alongside industry bodies to mitigate investor losses.

Directed the response to a JV partner insolvency at two shopping centre destinations — resetting the JV structure to enable step-in by lenders, resulting in successful co-management and business continuity.

Specialist Capability

Interim Executive Leadership

When experienced senior real estate leadership is required at short notice — covering a period of transition, a specific project or an unplanned departure — Stockbridge Asset Management is available for interim executive appointments across both private and public sector organisations.

John brings Director-level experience leading large, multi-disciplinary real estate teams — stepping in quickly, establishing credibility with existing teams and stakeholders, getting to grips with issues and maintaining momentum to minimise delays and risk whilst a permanent or longer-term solution is sourced.

Assignments may include interim asset management or estates roles, team stabilisation, project leadership, or senior oversight during a formal recruitment process.

Specialist Capability

Business Change & Transformation

Restructuring a property function, reviewing an operating model, or selecting and appointing managing agents and asset management operating partners are decisions with long-term financial and operational consequences — and ones that benefit from senior, independent oversight.

Stockbridge Asset Management works with clients to define the brief, stress-test the process and ensure the right outcome is achieved. Drawing on direct experience of overseeing major platform transformation and operating partner selection programmes, John brings a sharp commercial eye to pitch documents, proposals and recommended solutions — asking the right questions, challenging assumptions and ensuring the client's interests remain front and centre at every stage.

We are pleased to work alongside our client's legal and specialist advisors and subject experts for larger projects and are able to offer access to a wider network of associates.
